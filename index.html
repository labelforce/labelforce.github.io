Machine Learning supported Crowdsourcing of Image Labeling

LabelForce is a crowdsourced Labeling App. Instead of just letting users label all the images, we preprocess them with a convolutional autoencoder, that can assess the similarity between images without labels. Users get rewarded, if the majority of users labeled the image the same. If they're wrong, they get a penalty. This reward/penalty system is implemented with the decentralized blockchain technology ethereum. Ethereum is a new platform for building apps on the blockchain that is in its public beta phase. Instead of just letting users label all the data like crowdflower, with LabelForce the manual labeling is minimized by the preprocessing. We believe that LabelForce is currently the most cost effective and accurate way of labeling images.
